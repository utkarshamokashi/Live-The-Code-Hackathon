Come Unity

About the project

Online support groups give people living with various mental and physical disorder a safe, welcoming place to share experiences, discuss coping skills, and offer each other hope. With the support of fellow mates who are facing the same issues and friends, we are rapidly adding more groups to meet the urgent need for online support.

  • camera Objectives
  • favorite Meet the team

Support groups help such people to feel a sense of identification with other group members and feel that they along with others are getting benefited by sharing their experiences and identifying that they are not the only ones who are in trauma. Online support groups provide an alternative method of support for people in distress by linking people with similar issues. They have the potential to improve the access and delivery of support to a wide range of people, including some who would not seek face-to-face support at all. Our aim and objective is to spread positivity by helping people connect with each other.
